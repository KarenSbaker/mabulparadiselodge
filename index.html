Providing a shared lounge, Mabul Paradise Lodge offers family-run accommodations in Mabul Island. Guests can enjoy meals from the in-house restaurant. Mabul Paradise Lodge offers a sun terrace. It is a 1 hour to Semporna Pier and 25 minutes to Sipadan Island by boat. The nearest airport is Tawau Airport, 51 mi from the property. The property offers airport pick-up services at additional charges. Activities at the lodge include scuba diving. The in-house restaurant serves up a variety of Asian cuisine.
